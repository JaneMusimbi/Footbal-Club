About Amani FC

The Amani Youth FC (AYFC) is a grassroots club that taps into the power of football to help unearth, nurture and develop grassroots talent in our country.
Our vision is to be To become one of Kenya's greatest community-based football club, acclaimed for its fantastic people, facilities, and ability to produce
successful team and players' potential.

The club was established in the year 2012 and happened to be among the best clubs in the community by supporting young talents with the aim of guiding them to avoid drug abuse, gender-based violence and helping them in pressure education through mobilization of support from well-wishers
